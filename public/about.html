We’re produce obsessed.

With over a decade of produce prowess, we deliver a quality you just can’t find at the grocery store.

Who's Ned?

I’m a drum-playing husband, dad of two, avocado aficionado, and co-founder of The Alexander Fruit Company.

Each week, I pick your produce with impeccable standards. Every fruit or vegetable that lands on your doorstep has to pass the Ned Test first.

We're on a mission.

Just like you, we’ve been disappointed with what the grocery aisles have to offer.

Mealy apples. Desert-dry limes. Overpriced parsley.

We’ve tasted what farmers have to offer, and we're bringing it right to your doorstep, at a better price than the grocery store.

Our Story

10 years ago in the farmer’s market parking lot, we started our company delivering 25 paper bags of produce to friends and family.

Our business grew and we quickly became the favorite produce delivery service to over 400 happy families in the Triangle.

Today, we bring familes and businesses the freshest, most delicious fruits and veggies.
