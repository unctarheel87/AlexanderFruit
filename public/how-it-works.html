FAQ

How often do we get our delivery?
We deliver to Cary on Wednesdays and the rest of the Triangle on Thursdays, unless you want to skip a week (that’s fine by us!).
Where do you deliver?
We deliver to the greater Triangle area including Raleigh, Durham, Chapel Hill, Wake Forest, Knightdale, Garner, Clayton, Cary, Apex, and even parts of Pittsboro. ZERO FEES.
Am I locked into a contract?
Nope. You can cancel your weekly membership at any time (although we doubt you’ll want to!) by letting us know by the Sunday before midnight of the week you’d like to cancel.
Are there any extra fees?
No sign up fee. No delivery fees. No confusing deposits or surcharges. Just the price of your perfectly picked produce package.
Can I customize my package?
For now we don’t offer customized packages.
Can I skip a week?
You can skip any week by emailing us at hello@AlexanderFruit.com before Sunday at midnight. SKIP requests received after Sunday at MIDNIGHT automatically become part of our Give program. This means we discount your package $10 and donate your produce to a local family in need since we have likely already bought your food from our all-star farmers & vendors.
Is this organic?
We love to include some local and/or organic options when they’re available at the farmer’s market, but our biggest priority is top-tier, delicious produce at an unbeatable price.
What kind of fruits and veggies will I get?
The variety will change week-to-week depending on what’s available at the farmers market. Some items will be staples like leafy greens, root veggies, onions, apples and bananas. But many items will change weekly to keep your meals interesting.
